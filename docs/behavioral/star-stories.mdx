# STAR Stories

Behavioral interviews are easier when you prepare reusable stories instead of
memorizing full scripts.

## STAR structure

- Situation: what context did the interviewer need?
- Task: what were you responsible for?
- Action: what did you personally do?
- Result: what changed because of your work?

## Story bank

Prepare examples for:

- Conflict or disagreement
- Ambiguous project
- Technical leadership
- Mistake or failure
- Tight deadline
- Mentoring or collaboration

## Review pass

Each story should include enough technical and human detail to feel specific,
but it should still be short enough to tell in two or three minutes.
